The Monster



Big Monster Sandiwch


Ingredients


  • Steak 2 scoops
  • 4 bacon strips
  • 1 loaf Italian herbs and cheese bread
  • Green bell peppers
  • Red onions
  • Mayo 3 swipes
  • 2x mont chd cheese (shredded)

Directions

  1. Cut Bread in half hotdog style
  2. Apply steak bacon and cheese spread out evenly
  3. Toast
  4. Apply green bell peppers onions and mayo spread out evenly
  5. Close sandwich
  6. Cut in half
